# qvra commit title rules

Commit titles must be:
- professional
- exact
- role-aware
- non-hyped
- non-ceremonial
- immediately legible in history

## Preferred forms

- `initialize <repo> repository`
- `establish <repo> public surface`
- `publish <repo> <artifact>`
- `align <repo> <naming or route change>`
- `refine <repo> <specific surface>`
- `remove <repo> <obsolete or forbidden surface>`

## Avoid

- filler adjectives
- emotional tone
- slogans
- vague words like `stuff`, `things`, `update`, `misc`
- repeated vanity prefixes
- titles that sound bigger than the actual delta

## Standard

A good title should tell a future maintainer what changed without needing the diff open first.
